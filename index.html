About Us


Darbots 4100 is a FIRST Tech Challenge Team located in Rome, Georgia. Our team consists of 9th-12th grade students from Darlington School.

We are excited to participate in the new 2023-2024 Center Stage season and look forward to meeting everyone!

Check us out on Youtube! Darbots 4100


FTC Centerstage Scorer



FTC Centerstage Scorer is a simple-to-use app to score practice matches for the 2023-2024 FIRST Tech Challenge season. The scoring is only for one alliance or bot at a time.

This app is developed by our team member and the source code will be on the Darbots GitHub.
